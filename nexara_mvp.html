âš™ Dual-Track Emission Mechanics
Each Bittensor block, validators dispatch queries to all miners requiring a 6-field structured response: price, yield, credit risk (Track A) + defect density, security score, financial impact (Track B). Miners are scored on both tracks independently, then combined as 60% Track A + 40% Track B into a Nexara composite score. TAO emission flows proportionally through Yuma consensus stake-weighted median aggregation. Poor performers earn less; dishonest validators are clipped.
